Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana (PMKMY)

Under this scheme, small and marginal farmers will receive a minimum assured pension of Rs. 3000 per month after attaining the age of 60 years.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Farmers between the ages of 18 to 40 years are eligible to apply.
  • They should have cultivable land holdings of up to 2 hectares.

Documents required:
  • Aadhar Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • Landholding Documents
