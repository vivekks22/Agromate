National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)

The e-NAM (National Agriculture Market) is a pan-India electronic trading portal that networks the existing APMC mandis to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities.

Key Features:

  • Single-window system for all APMC-related services.
  • Real-time price discovery based on supply and demand.
  • Reduced transaction costs and time.
  • Transparent online bidding process.

Benefits:
  • Empowers farmers to sell their produce directly to consumers.
  • Eliminates intermediaries, ensuring better prices for farmers.
  • Promotes competitive and transparent trading practices.
