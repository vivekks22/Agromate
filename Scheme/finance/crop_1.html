Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana

Under this scheme, financial aid of Rs. 50,000 per hectare/ three years is allowed, out of which Rs. 31,000 (61 percent) is given to the farmer directly.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • All farmers eligible for this scheme.
  • A group or cluster of farmers also eligible for this scheme.
  • Each Cluster or group must have 50 farmers willing to take up organic farming are eligible.

Documents required:
  • Applicant's Aadhaar card
  • Applicant's bank account details
  • Applicant's passport size photos if required
