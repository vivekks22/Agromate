PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

Under this scheme, farmers would be given Rs.6000 per year in 3 installments of Rs. 2000 each.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Farmers belonging to any Indian state are eligible under this scheme.
  • Farmers must have a Savings Account (or Jan-Dhan Account) .
  • Multi-tasking staff, Class IV and Group D government employees are eligible.

Documents required:
  • Kisan Credit Card
  • Bank Passbook
  • Aadhar Card
