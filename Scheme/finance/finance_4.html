Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) is a national mission for financial inclusion to ensure access to financial services, banking facilities, insurance coverage, and pension schemes for all households in the country.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • All Indian citizens aged 10 years and above are eligible to open an account under this scheme.
  • No minimum balance is required.
  • Basic banking facilities are provided with the account.

Documents required:
  • Any valid identification proof such as Aadhar card, Voter ID, PAN card, or passport.

Toll-Free Helpline Number:
  • Number: 1800-11-0001
  • Email: pmjdy@nic.in

More Information: pmjdy.gov.in
