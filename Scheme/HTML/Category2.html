Financial Support Schemes

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

Farmers with less than two hectares of landholding will get up to Rs. 6,000 per year as a minimum income support.

Read More

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maandhan Yojana(PMKMY)

Marginalised farmers will get a minimum pension of Rs 3000 / month on attaining the age of 60 years.

Read More

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Pension Yojana

Under this, A farmer will have to contribute Rs 55 per month and the Central Government will make an equal contribution and he will get a pension of Rs 3000 per month after the age of 60.

Read More

Jan Dhan Yojana

This scheme ensures access to financial services, namely, a basic savings & deposit accounts, remittance, credit, insurance, pension in an affordable manner.

National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)

eNAM is a pan-India electronic trading portal which networks the existing APMC mandis to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities.
