Prime Minister Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana

This is a Life Insurance scheme with risk coverage of Rs. 2 Lakh in case of death of the insured, due to any reason.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Farmers must have a Savings Account (or Jan-Dhan Account).
  • Farmers belonging to any Indian state are eligible under this scheme.
  • The Criteria of selection of Implementing Agency and area allocation will be same as PMFBY.

Documents required:
  • Bank Passbook
  • Aadhar Card
