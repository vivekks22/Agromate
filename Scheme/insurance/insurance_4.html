Livestock insurance Scheme

This scheme provides protection mechanism to the farmers against any eventual loss of their animals due to death.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Farmers belonging to any Indian state are eligible under this scheme.
  • Farmers must have a Savings Account (or Jan-Dhan Account).

Documents required:
  • Bank Passbook
  • Aadhar Card
