Crop Field Schemes

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Financial aid of Rs. 50,000 per hectare/ three years is allowed, out of which Rs. 31,000 (61 percent) is given to the farmer directly.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

Focuses on creating sources for assured irrigation, also creating protective irrigation by harnessing rain water.

Micro Irrigation Fund (MIF)

Provide states with concessional rate of interest on required amount in order to promote micro-irrigation.

New Jal Shakti Ministry

To provide piped water connection to every Indian household by the year 2024.

Soil Health Card Scheme

Under this, the govt plans to issue soil cards to farmers which will carry crop-wise recommendations of nutrients and fertilisers required for the individual farms.

To boost the growth of wheat and paddy, and reduce the black marketeering and hoarding or urea.
