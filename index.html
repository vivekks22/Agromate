Schemes

The place for all the Government schemes providing support to the farmers, starting from providing financial aid for building their houses to developing their crop fields for a better living.
MSP

MSP is the guaranteed ‘minimum floor price’ that farmer must get from the government in case the market price of the crops falls below the MSP.
Insect Finder

An innovative tool tailored for farmers to easily identify insects by simply uploading a picture. Whether you're facing a pest problem or curious about beneficial bugs in your fields, our user-friendly interface provides quick and accurate insect identification.
Weather

Designed specifically for farmers, our Weather Forecast service offers vital information essential for agricultural planning. Stay ahead of the weather with precise details on humidity levels, current weather data, atmospheric pressure, air quality ratings, wind speed, and direction. Monitor air quality to safeguard crops and livestock, while also tracking sunrise and sunset times to optimize work schedules.
